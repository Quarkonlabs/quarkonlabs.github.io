Who We Are

Quarkon Laboratories is an **innovation-driven research lab** dedicated to **neural prosthetics and AI-driven robotics**. Inspired by the **Emily May Foundation**, we work toward making prosthetics more affordable, smarter, and accessible to those who need them most.

Our Mission

At Quarkon, we believe that technology should be a force for **empowerment**. We are dedicated to **removing financial barriers** and **creating groundbreaking assistive devices** that improve lives.
